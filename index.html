About the lab

The NERVE Lab at the University of Vermont is interested in cognitive development, particularly as it relates to substance use and risk-taking behaviors.

Much of our work levereges Adolescent Brain Cognitive Development (ABCD Study), IMAGEN Study, and ENIGMA-Addiction working group data to study brain development at unprecedented scales.

The NERVE Lab is led by Hugh Garavan and Alexi Potter.
